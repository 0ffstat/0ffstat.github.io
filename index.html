About Us

Established in 2015, Highpoint carries out work on plant and animal pest control projects including ecological surveys and biosecurity incursion responses.

They carry out work in a wide range of environments including high profile reserves and the islands of the Hauraki Gulf and capable of carrying out these works in diffcult to access locations as experienced rope access operators.

Highpoint is owned and operated by Joel Leong and Nicole Taylor with the aim of creating a small mobile work unit, able to respond quickly to client requests. Between them, all management, administration and field work tasks are carried out to the highest standard.

They continue to set the standard in the delivery of specialised conservation projects, especially those requiring higher levels of communication with clients, stakeholders and members of the public and excel in the use of GIS and data collection systems for the creation of detailled, practical reports.
